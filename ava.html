Creatures of Ava

Gameplay Programmer & Environment Artist

Unreal Engine 5 • Blueprint • C++

Creatures of Ava is an exciting action-adventure, creature-saver game inviting you to explore a world brimming with wild beings. Understand and tame the creatures of Ava and let them lead you through a variety of ecosystems, all in the hopes of saving the planet from a life-consuming infection.

Inverge Studios
